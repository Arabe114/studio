Welcome to your English Learning Guide!

This guide provides a few tips and common phrases to help you on your journey to mastering English.

---

**Section 1: Common Greetings**

*   Hello / Hi - A general greeting.
*   Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening - Greetings for specific times of day.
*   How are you? - A common way to ask about someone's well-being.
    *   Response: "I'm fine, thank you. And you?"

---

**Section 2: Basic Phrases**

*   Thank you / Thanks - Expressing gratitude.
*   You're welcome - A polite response to "Thank you."
*   Please - Used to make a polite request.
*   Excuse me - To get someone's attention or to apologize for a minor offense.
*   I'm sorry - To apologize.

---

**Section 3: Tips for Learning**

1.  Practice every day, even if it's just for 15 minutes.
2.  Listen to English music and podcasts.
3.  Watch movies and TV shows in English with subtitles.
4.  Don't be afraid to make mistakes. It's part of learning!
5.  Try to think in English.

---

Happy learning!
